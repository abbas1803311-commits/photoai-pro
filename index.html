Under the hood

Two models, one upload.

Restoration

A face- and texture-aware model trained on degraded photographs. It separates genuine damage — scratches, mold, creases, faded dye — from the picture underneath, then rebuilds only what's missing.

Upscaling

A super-resolution model increases pixel dimensions while inferring plausible fine detail, so edges stay crisp instead of smearing into blur at 4K sizes.

Your files

Images are sent securely to processing, returned to your browser, and not used for training. Nothing is stored longer than it takes to process your request.

Questions

Good to know.

What file types can I upload?

JPG, PNG and WEBP, up to 12MB. For best restoration results, upload the highest-resolution scan of the original you have access to.

Can I use both modes on the same photo?

Yes. Run Restore Old Photo first to repair damage, download the result, then upload it again and run Enhance & Upscale to 4K.

Is there a size limit on the 4K output?

Output is capped at 3840×2160px (4K UHD). Very small or very large source images may be scaled to stay within that bound.

Do you keep my photos?

No. Images pass through processing and are discarded once your result is returned.
